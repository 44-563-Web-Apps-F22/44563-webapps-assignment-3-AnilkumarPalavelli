Phillie Phantic is the official mascot for the Philadelphia Phillies Major League Baseball team. He is a large, furry, green bipedal flightless bird with an extendable tongue.

Here is the Some Interesting Facts about the Phillie Phantic

  1. The Phillie Phanatic is the official mascot of the Philadelphia Phillies. He is a pleasingly plump furry green creature with a long cylindrical beak containing an even longer tongue that sticks out. He was created by Wayde Erickson and Bonnie Harrison who designed and built Miss Piggy. The character is named for the fanatical fans of the team and was created to bring more families to Veterans Stadium, the Phillies ballpark at the time.
  2. The Phillie Phanatic costume, along with Youppi! and the San Diego Chicken, are the only mascots on display at in the National Baseball Hall of Fame and Museum in Cooperstown, NY.
  3. The Phillies Phanatic is Phildelphias famous face ambassador of good cheer. Our 10th Man on the Field. He has never said a word and I like his pitch. He is a Very Important Person.
